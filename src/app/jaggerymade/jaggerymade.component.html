Product Description
Jaggery Pootharekulu are a traditional Andhra sweet made by layering rice flour batter with a sweet filling of jaggery and ghee. These treats boast a delightful combination of crispy textures and rich, caramel-like sweetness, making them a beloved indulgence during festivals and celebrations in Andhra Pradesh, India.
Ingredients: Rice starch, Jaggery, Ghee
Nutritional Facts:
  • Calories: 150-200 kcal
  • Total Fat: 5-8 grams
  • Total Carbohydrates: 25-30 grams
  • Sugars: 15-20 grams
  • Protein: 1-2 grams
Health Benefits:
The inherent goodness of rice, enriched with essential micronutrients, particularly vitamin B1, contributes to enhancing memory power and concentration. Furthermore, the inclusion of jaggery in our Bellam Pootharekulu serves as a natural boost to hemoglobin levels, promoting healthy blood circulation.
Product storage:
To preserve the authentic taste and freshness of our Godavari district's Authentic Sweet Bellam Pootharekulu, ensure to store them in a sealed container immediately upon opening the package. While these jaggery paper sweets maintain their quality for up to three weeks from the date of opening or manufacture, their flavor is at its peak when consumed within six weeks.
