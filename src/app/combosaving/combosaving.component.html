Product Description
Indulge in the ultimate treat with our Combo Combination Pootharekulu, featuring a delightful blend of flavors and textures. This special offering includes a variety of Pootharekulu made with Bellam (jaggery), Sugar, and an assortment of wholesome dry fruits. Each bite promises a symphony of tastes, from the rich sweetness of jaggery to the classic sweetness of sugar, complemented by the crunchy goodness of dry fruits.
Ingredients: Rice flour, Sugar, Jaggery, Ghee, Assorted dry fruits (such as almonds, cashews, pistachios, raisins, etc.)
Nutritional Facts:
  • Calories: 150-200 kcal
  • Total Fat: 5-8 grams
  • Total Carbohydrates: 25-30 grams
  • Sugars: 15-20 grams
  • Protein: 1-2 grams
Health Benefits:
Our Combo Combination Pootharekulu offer not only indulgent flavors but also a variety of health benefits. The inclusion of Bellam provides essential minerals like iron and potassium, while sugar adds a familiar sweetness. Assorted dry fruits contribute vital nutrients such as vitamins, minerals, and healthy fats, promoting overall well-being. With a balance of carbohydrates, fats, and proteins, these treats offer a satisfying snack option for any time of day.
Product storage:
To maintain the freshness and flavor of our Combo Combination Pootharekulu, store them in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. Avoid exposure to moisture or direct sunlight to preserve their delicate texture and taste.
