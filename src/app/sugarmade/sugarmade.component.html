Product Description
Savor the irresistible sweetness of our Sugar Pootharekulu, a beloved delicacy hailing from Andhra Pradesh, India. Crafted with care and tradition, each bite of these crispy treats offers a delightful fusion of textures and flavors, making them a cherished indulgence for any occasion.
Ingredients: Rice starch, Sugar, Ghee
Nutritional Facts:
  • Calories: 150-200 kcal
  • Total Fat: 5-8 grams
  • Total Carbohydrates: 25-30 grams
  • Sugars: 15-20 grams
  • Protein: 1-2 grams
Health Benefits:
Indulging in our Sugar Pootharekulu offers more than just a delicious treat; it also provides several health benefits. Made primarily from rice flour, these treats offer a source of carbohydrates for quick energy. With minimal saturated fat and cholesterol, they make for a guilt-free indulgence. Additionally, the presence of ghee provides a touch of richness while keeping the treats light and crispy. While high in sugars, they are free from cholesterol and offer a modest amount of dietary fiber, promoting digestive health.
Product storage:
To ensure the longevity of our Sugar Pootharekulu and preserve their delicate texture and flavor, store them in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. Avoid exposure to moisture or direct sunlight, as it may affect the quality of the treats.
