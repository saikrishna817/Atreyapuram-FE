Atreyapuram

Atreyapuram is a small village located in East Godavari district of Andhra Pradesh, India. It's well known for its breadth taking sceneris and green surroundings. Atreyapuram is Particularly famous for its mouth watering Papersweets famous known as Atreyapuram Pootharekulu.

Preparation

Atreyapuram Putharekulu is a signature sweet from the town of Atreyapuram. The preparation of Atreyapuram Putharekulu is a traditional art, passed down through generations. The main ingredients include rice flour, jaggery (unrefined sugar), ghee, and sometimes cardamom for added flavor.

The unique aspect of Putharekulu lies in the delicate and labor-intensive process of creating the thin layers. The rice batter is spread thinly on a cloth, dried, and then interleaved with layers of jaggery and ghee.The stack is carefully rolled into a cylindrical shape and sliced into individual servings.The end result is a crispy, flaky sweet with a rich, caramelized taste. They are known for their distinct texture, combining the crispiness of the layers with the sweetness of the jaggery, creating a delightful culinary experience that reflects the culinary craftsmanship of Atreyapuram.
Visitors often purchase these sweets as a part of their cultural exploration of the region or as gifts for friends and family. The popularity of Atreyapuram Putharekulu has made it a symbol of the culinary heritage of Andhra Pradesh. These delectable treats are not only popular in the local region but have gained fame across the state and beyond. The name "Putharekulu" is derived from the Telugu words "Putha" meaning layer and "Reku" meaning sheet, reflecting the intricate layering process involved in making this sweet.Atreyapuram Putharekulu are often enjoyed during festivals, special occasions, and as souvenirs for those visiting the town.

Over the years, the women of Atreyapuram village have mastered the art of making the most traditional, rich and delicious sweet “Pootharekulu“, which is now the most sought-after sweet for every occasion in Andhra Pradesh.
