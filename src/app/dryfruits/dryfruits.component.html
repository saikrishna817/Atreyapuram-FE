Product Description
Experience the exquisite taste and wholesome goodness of our Dry Fruit Pootharekulu, available in both Sugar and Jaggery variations. These delightful Andhra sweets are meticulously crafted to offer a perfect blend of crunchy texture and rich flavors, making them a cherished indulgence for any occasion.
Ingredients: Rice flour, Sugar or Jaggery, Ghee, Assorted dry fruits (such as almonds, cashews, pistachios, raisins, etc.)
Nutritional Facts:
  • Calories: 150-200 kcal
  • Total Fat: 5-8 grams
  • Total Carbohydrates: 25-30 grams
  • Sugars: 15-20 grams
  • Protein: 1-2 grams
Health Benefits:
Our Dry Fruit Pootharekulu, whether sweetened with sugar or jaggery, provide not only a delicious treat but also a dose of essential nutrients. The inclusion of assorted dry fruits adds a nutritional boost, providing vitamins, minerals, and healthy fats. While offering a source of carbohydrates for energy, these treats are relatively low in cholesterol and sodium. Additionally, they contain a modest amount of dietary fiber, promoting digestive health and satiety.
Product storage:
To preserve the freshness and flavor of our Dry Fruit Pootharekulu, store them in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. Avoid exposure to moisture or direct sunlight to maintain their delicate texture and taste.
